---
title: Workspaces and Projects
description: Organize durable agent outputs for individuals and teams.
---

## Owner Namespace

Artifacts live under an owner namespace. The owner can be a person or a workspace, and the URL keeps that namespace visible.

```txt
/{ownerUsername}/{projectSlug}/{artifactSlug}
```

## Projects

Projects are the primary grouping mechanism for artifacts. They make URLs readable and give agents a predictable target for publishing related work.

Use separate projects for:

- A product area.
- A customer or incident investigation.
- A recurring report workflow.
- A design exploration or prototype set.

## Workspaces

Workspaces let teams share ownership of projects and artifacts. A useful personal artifact can move into a team context when it becomes shared source material.

## Invitations

Workspace and team invitation flows are handled in the web app. Invited members inherit access through workspace membership, while artifact-specific policies still apply for sensitive outputs.

## Slugs

Project and artifact slugs should be stable, human-readable identifiers. Agents can check slug availability before creating projects or artifacts, and pushed artifacts retry on slug conflicts where the CLI supports it.
